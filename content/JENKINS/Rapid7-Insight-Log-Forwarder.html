Jenkins : Rapid7 Insight Log Forwarder

Created by Unknown User (donatello_rapid7) on Jul 02, 2018

 

Jenkins plug-in for Rapid7 Insight platform.

Usage

  • Create a token based input on Rapid7 Insight
  • Enable the "Rapid7 Insight Forwarder" in the "Build Environment" section of your job configuration.
  • Copy the token generated by InsightOps into the "Token" field:

  • Now the console output will be sent to your log on InsightOps and you can search and analyze it to your heart's content.
