No information for the plugin 'emotional-hudson' is available. It may have been removed from distribution.

This funny plugin changes the expression of Mr. Hudson in the background when your builds fail.

The plugin is maintained by Masanobu Imai and is hosted on its own project site at Google Code. Also check Kohsuke's introduction of this plugin.

Once installed, you need to enable this on per-job basis.
