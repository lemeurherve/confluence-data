Jenkins : Hudson Helper iPhone and iPod Touch App

Created by Unknown User (dgreen99@gmail.com), last modified on May 28, 2009

With this application you can monitor your CI builds right from your iPhone or iPod Touch. Hudson Helper uses Hudson's remote XML API in order to provide detailed information about your builds. Network usage is minimal due to the use of caching and local data storage.

More details at Hudson Helper: Hudson On Your iPhone

Installation

You can get Hudson Helper by searching for 'Hudson Helper' in the app store, or click here to get it with iTunes.
