Jenkins : FOSDEM 2013

Created by Unknown User (orrc), last modified by Unknown User (rtyler) on Jan 09, 2014

Jenkins will be at FOSDEM, in Brussels, on February 2-3rd, 2013!

See the mailing list if you're interested in helping with coordination

Activities

  • Unknown User (kohsuke) will give a keynote talk on How we made the Jenkins community on Saturday at 11:00
  • Some project members are organizing (and will give talks in) the Testing and Automation devroom, all-day Saturday
  • There will be a Jenkins meetup & discussion for users and developers, plus a toast to Jenkins' second birthday!
  • Like last year, we have a stand for both days (K building, first floor) where we will spread the word about Jenkins, hand out stickers, give demos etc...

Manning the stand

The Jenkins stand was very popular last year. We met a lot of interesting existing users, convinced people to become future users, gave away a ton of stickers, and had a great time.
This year we intend to do the same. Doing so requires keeping the stand occupied by preferably two people across Saturday and Sunday, so we need volunteers to help out for a shift or two!
