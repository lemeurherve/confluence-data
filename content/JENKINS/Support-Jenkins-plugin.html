Future-proof your Hudson installation by supporting Jenkins

Usage

This is a plugin you can install on Hudson (and not Jenkins) to ensure that you'll be able to upgrade to Jenkins when you are ready.

When you install this plugin,

  1. The wording in the page footer is altered to indicate that your Hudson will soon be Jenkins
  2. You can optionally also replace the title logo in the top-left corner to that of Jenkins (via the system config page.)
  3. Update center is replaced to that of Jenkins
