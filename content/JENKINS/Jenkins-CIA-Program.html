Warning!

Jenkins CIA program has been closed on January 2016 as it duplicates Jenkins Area Meetup. Please follow the guidelines on the referenced page if you want to setup/attend a Jenkins-related meetup in your region. This page is just a tombstone with a cool icon.

What was Jenkins CIA?

Jenkins CIA stands for "Jenkins CI Ambassador", and is modeled after street teams. We call the street team members "CIA agents". The program logo is shown in the right. The goal was to help/empower those who go to local meetups and spread the words of Jenkins.

In September 2015 the community announced the Jenkins Area Meetup program, which completely replaces the Jenkins CIA program. Since Jenkins is not only about CI, Jenkins CIA term is outdated as well.
