What's this?

This plugin publishes build records over to Google Calendar.



This plugin needs to know your calendar XML feed address to push data to Google calendar. You can find this URL through the following steps:

  1. Go to Google Calendar
  2. Click the triangle icon on the right of the calendar you would like to use
  3. Select "Calendar Settings"
  4. Click on either of the two XML buttons and copy the link
